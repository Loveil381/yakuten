## Absolute Prohibitions

HRT Pharmacopoeia will never publish any of the following:

<DangerBox>
- Personalized prescription advice ("you should take X mg")
- Drug purchase links, sales agents, or referral/commission QR codes
- Wording that weakens recognition of medical emergencies (DVT, hepatotoxicity, meningioma, etc.)
- Medical claims without a DOI or citation
- Third-party tracking scripts or storage of user health data
- Configurations that bypass AI disclaimers or safety warnings
</DangerBox>

## Language and Tone

- **Avoid absolutes**: prefer "we suggest / typically / in most cases" over "must / always / never"
- **Address the reader directly**: write "you can choose A or B" rather than "patients should choose A"
- **State uncertainty plainly**: conclusions at evidence level B/C/X must be tagged with "data are limited" or "expert opinion"
- **Reduce stigma**: use community-affirming language and avoid outdated or overly clinical terms

## Citations and Copyright

- Every medical claim is sourced via the `CitationRef` component (IDs map to entries in `src/data/references.json`)
- DOIs are preferred; where no DOI exists, cite journal publication info plus a stable URL
- Figures and diagrams are original or sourced from Creative Commons / public domain works; reproductions are credited
- Original site content is released under CC BY-SA 4.0; code is governed by the repository LICENSE

## Error Correction

Readers can submit reports via the [feedback form](https://wj.qq.com/s2/16576000/5fe5/) or [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/cantascendia/yakuten/issues):

| Type | Response time | Handling |
|---|---|---|
| Factual error | Initial reply within 72 hours | **Erratum notice** at the top of the page + change log |
| Citation drift | 72 hours | Replace the citation and publish a public correction log |
| Outdated information | 7 days | Incremental update + refresh `lastReviewed` |
| Dangerous advice | 24-hour emergency takedown | Repair or keep offline pending review |

Major corrections are also propagated to:

- Version history at `/en/appendix-references/`
- RSS / Telegram / email subscribers

## Complaints and Appeals

- **Content complaints**: [feedback form](https://wj.qq.com/s2/16576000/5fe5/)
- **Legal / copyright**: open a GitHub Issue with the `legal` label, or contact the repository maintainers
- **Privacy**: this site does not collect personally identifiable information; any reported privacy leak is handled within 24 hours

## Conflict-of-Interest Disclosure

- **No commercial sponsorship, no drug advertising, no purchase commissions**
- Operating costs are covered by volunteer contributions and open-source community donations
- Editors and medical advisors sign conflict-of-interest disclosures; topics with directly related prescribing or commercial relationships are recused
- No external links carry affiliate identifiers; only community-vetted hospital and hotline resources are listed

## Review Cycle

| Page type | Default review interval |
|---|---|
| Emergency recognition, dosing red lines, danger warnings | **6 months** |
| Drug monographs and guides | **12 months** |
| Tools, policies, methodology | **12 months** |
| Blog posts | Verified once 12 months after publication |

A major guideline change (EMA/FDA safety alert, new WPATH / Endocrine Society edition) triggers an **incremental update within 48 hours**.

<WarningBox>
This page is a public commitment to editorial self-governance. **If you find us violating any clause above, please call it out publicly** — that accountability is the only way we keep faith with our readers.
</WarningBox>
