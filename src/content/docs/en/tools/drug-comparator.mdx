## Medication Comparison

Select 2-3 medications to quickly compare their key parameters.

**This tool helps you understand:**

- VTE risk differences between estrogen routes (oral vs transdermal vs injection)
- Monitoring requirements and contraindications for anti-androgens
- Evidence levels and dosage ranges for each medication

<DrugComparator client:visible />

## How to Interpret Results

- **VTE Risk (RR)**: Relative risk value. RR=1.0 is baseline; >1.5 indicates significant increase
- **Evidence Level**: A = RCT/Meta-analysis, B = Single RCT/Cohort study, C = Case report/Expert opinion
- **Monitoring**: Lab tests required during use of the medication
