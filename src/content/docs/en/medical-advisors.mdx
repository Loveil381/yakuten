## Review Team Structure

HRT Pharmacopoeia's medical content is reviewed by a cross-disciplinary volunteer team, assigned by topic:

| Domain | Review responsibility |
|---|---|
| Endocrinology | Estrogen / antiandrogen / progestin dosing, blood test interpretation, metabolism, and bone density |
| Psychiatry | Gender-affirming assessment, mood fluctuation, interactions with concurrent medications |
| Hepatology / Hematology | CPA hepatotoxicity, VTE risk, coagulation monitoring |
| Legal advisors | Mainland China prescribing compliance, privacy regulation, traveling internationally with medication |

**Current status (2026-04-19)**

The site is currently in a **"community curation + cross-referenced international guidelines"** phase, and **has not yet contracted publicly named licensed physicians**.

- All medical content is anchored to the following authoritative sources, each cited with a DOI:
  - Endocrine Society 2017, *Endocrine Treatment of Gender-Dysphoric/Gender-Incongruent Persons*
  - WPATH SOC 8 (2022)
  - UCSF Transgender Care Guidelines
  - EMA / FDA drug safety alerts
- Every medical claim must be accompanied by a `<CitationRef>` pointing to the primary source
- Page frontmatter mandates the three metadata fields `evidenceLevel`, `lastReviewed`, and `references`

**Next steps**: We are reaching out to trans-friendly licensed physicians in mainland China, Hong Kong / Macau / Taiwan, Japan, and North America to join a rotating review pool. If you are interested, please contact us via the [feedback form](https://wj.qq.com/s2/16576000/5fe5/) or GitHub.

## Review Principles

- **No prescribing relationship**: reviewers have no direct prescribing relationship to the medications or brands recommended on this site, to avoid conflicts of interest
- **Dissent on file**: review comments and editorial decisions are recorded throughout; major disagreements are publicly disclosed
- **Identity protection**: advisors may remain anonymous, but must provide the editorial team with a copy of their professional license and proof of affiliation
- **Update obligation**: within 48 hours of a major guideline update, reviewers confirm whether existing pages are affected
