## Important Notes

<div class="warning-box">
<div class="warning-box__title">⚠ Not a Medical Diagnosis</div>

This tool **cannot replace** professional medical evaluation. Risk scores are based on known population statistics and cannot reflect your complete personal health status. Please share your assessment results with your healthcare provider as a starting point for discussion.

**High risk does not mean you cannot use HRT** — it means you may need more frequent monitoring, different administration routes, or specific preventive measures.
